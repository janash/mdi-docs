# STRESS



## <STRESS
Receive virial stress tensor

**Datatype:** `MDI_DOUBLE`  
**Quantity**: `9`  

 Engine sends its virial stress tensor to the driver.

## >STRESS
Send virial stress tensor

**Datatype:** `MDI_DOUBLE`  
**Quantity**: `9`  

 Driver sends virial stress tensor to engine, replacing its internal tensor.
